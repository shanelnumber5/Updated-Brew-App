Willie’s Distillery is a small batch distillery located on Main street in picturesque Ennis, Montana, on the banks of the fabled Madison River. Our mission is to make World Class Spirits for World Class Individuals.

We use fresh mountain water straight from the source, and find our grains and ingredients from small Montana farms and growers ranging from a local apiary to the barley fields of the Montana Highline. Each bottle is handled and filled with care.

They say the smaller the town, the more time slows down. We’re ok with that because you just can’t rush perfection.
