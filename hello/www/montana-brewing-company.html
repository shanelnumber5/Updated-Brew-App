Welcome to Montana Brewing Company located in downtown Billings! Voted Billings best lunch for six years in a row. We have beautiful, locally brewed, hand-crafted beer! We are proud of everything Montana has to offer which is why we provide locally made drinks from many Montana distilleries. Our dynamic menu offers a wide selection of food options along with a full bar for individual drink preferences! Our dining area presents a great option for family members of all ages! Food is served from 11am to 11pm every day. Happy Hour is Monday-Friday from 4pm-6pm. Anything else, just give us a call or check out our Facebook Page! We hope to see you soon!
