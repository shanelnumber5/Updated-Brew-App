Founded in Bozeman, Montana in 2013, Bridger Brewing was born from a love of beer, an appreciation of good food and the desire to create a community gathering place. With locations in Bozeman and Three Forks, we are rooted in places we love and thirsty for new adventures.

Inspired by the pioneer spirits of those who came before us, three men set out to create a place for people to gather, share a pint and a story. The idea was simple: brew good, do good. Now, with two locations, live events and an ever-growing menu of daring beers, the Bridger crew is just as excited to share a pint with you as we were on the day we opened.
