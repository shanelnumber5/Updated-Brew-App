Wildwood Brewery

Wildwood Brewery's Website

Wildwood Brewing is not your average brewery, in Montana or otherwise. From the stunning lighted entry gate to the iconic Montana characters dropping by from the distinguished beers on tap to the handcrafted wooden sign swinging high above the four-vessel brew house log book. Every element of Wildwood Brewing has a unique story.

Less than five minutes into a conversation with owner and brewmaster Jim Lueders it will dawn on you that you are speaking to a man with great respect for both historic magnitude and true forward-thinking innovation. Wildwood Brewery represents this and more. Come on in, grab a seat by the fire, and listen to a story told by a fine craft beer from Montana
