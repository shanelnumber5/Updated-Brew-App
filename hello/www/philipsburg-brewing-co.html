We opened Philipsburg Brewing Company in 2012 with the idea of providing quality, hand-crafted beers in a friendly, unique setting. OUR GOAL IS TO BE A SELF-SUSTAINING BREWING COMPANY THAT ENRICHES THE LIVES OF THE COMMUNITY WE SERVE AND THE PEOPLE WE EMPLOY. WE WANT TO BE A BUSINESS STAFF AND CUSTOMERS ARE PROUD TO SUPPORT.

Philipsburg Brewing uses only the finest Montana malt, local mountain spring water and exclusively whole cone hops to craft our beer. Mike Elliott, Head Brewer, with the support of Ben Johnson, Lead Brewer & Recipe Developer, have quickly earned a reputation for creating unique, crisp, award winning lagers and ales.

We invite you to come visit the brewery, but if you can't at least browse through our website, find out where our beers are served, then plan a trip to Pburg in the near future. Our brewery is located in downtown Philipsburg, Montana in a historic bank building constructed in 1888. We are open 7 days a week and feature live music on Friday and Saturday night.
