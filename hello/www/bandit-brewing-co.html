Bandit Brewing Co. is nano brewery nestled at the base of the Bitterroot Valley in Darby, Mt. Bandit Brewery brews 40 gallon batches at a time and is one of the smallest breweries in Montana. Small batch production allows Bandit Brewery to provide a constantly changing tap list of craft beer to enjoy in a small comfortable tasting room just off I-93 in Downtown Darby
