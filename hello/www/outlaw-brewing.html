Outlaw is your friendly neighborhood brewery. We are located behind the Target shopping center on North 27th amidst a large and blossoming residential area. We have the capacity to produce both large and small batches which allows us the freedom to explore and experiment with many different beer styles. We typically have 11 beers on tap in our tasting room at all times.
