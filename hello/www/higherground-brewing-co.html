We use the highest quality ingredients, regardless of the cost, in order to serve you the best possible product. You’ll taste the difference! We make our pizza dough fresh daily, using high quality Montana flour, and pride ourselves in the crust it produces. We also source as many of our other ingredients locally as possible, and hope you enjoy the results

Higherground’s mainstay brews are Dry Fly IPA, Clearwater Crystal Ale, Hurruh Scotch Ale, At Ease Golden Ale, Flash Flood Milk Stout, Hula Hopped IPA and Base Camp Irish Red.
