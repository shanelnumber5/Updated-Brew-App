Conflux is a brewpub/bar in downtown Missoula, MT and was built around simple ideas: Let's create a convergence (conflux) of great beer and unique food using sustainable and community driven practices.

Take in the view from the oversized windows and our open balcony on the amazing corner of Pattee St and Main St in downtown Missoula. Inside you can check out a full view of the brewery operation with our version of classic beer styles and Montana inspired comfort food. Outside you can hang out on our patios, drink by our fire pit and enjoy sidewalk dining along Pattee St
