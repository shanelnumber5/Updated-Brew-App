Big Spring Brewing is housed inside the historic Central Feed Mercantile building in downtown Lewistown, Montana. Our brewery fresh craft beers are always on tap at our neighboring steakhouse, Central Feed Grilling Company. You will appreciate their friendly, warm, and inviting atmosphere while you enjoy a refreshing Big Spring Brewing beer.

We strive to bring quality and craftsmanship to every beer we brew. We take pride in sourcing local ingredients, using 2-row barley grown in Montana and pristine water sourced from the local Big Snowy Mountains to brew our craft beer.
